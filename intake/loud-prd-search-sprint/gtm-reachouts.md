## Customization Tips

1. **Research the company** - Mention their specific product or a recent announcement
2. **Reference a pain point** - If they've posted about integration challenges, mention it
3. **Name the data sources** - Be specific about which integrations matter for their vertical
4. **Social proof** - If you have relevant conversations/design partners, mention the pattern
5. **Keep it short** - Founders get 100+ emails/day. Under 150 words.

---

## What to Avoid

- Don't say "AI-powered" or "revolutionary" — they're building AI, they don't need hype
- Don't oversell — we're looking for design partners, not closing deals
- Don't be vague — name specific data sources and problems
- Don't attach PDFs — if they're interested, they'll ask for more info
