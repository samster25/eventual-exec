# Borys Aptekar — Expert Interview Questions

**Background:** PM for GTM AI at ClickUp, previously Growth Systems Architect. Deep RevOps/systems experience across ClickUp, Instabase, Motive, AppDynamics, VMware. Currently building AI into GTM workflows (automated inbound, NL quoting, AI-driven MEDDPICC). Understands both the systems architecture and the business outcomes.

**Goal:** Understand how GTM teams research accounts today, where external context is missing, and how AI is changing the workflow. Borys is uniquely positioned because he's building GTM AI tools internally at ClickUp right now.

---

## Opening (2 min)

- Thanks for the time. We're building tools for GTM teams and trying to understand the workflow deeply before we build the wrong thing. I'd love to learn from what you're seeing at ClickUp and across your career.

## His current work at ClickUp (10 min)

1. You're building AI into GTM at ClickUp — automated inbound, NL quoting, MEDDPICC eval. Can you walk me through what prompted those projects? What pain were you solving first?

2. For the automated inbound lead handling — what does that look like end to end? Where does the AI add the most value vs. where does a human still need to step in?

3. On the MEDDPICC deal evaluation — where does the data come from? Is it mostly internal (call recordings, CRM fields, emails) or are you pulling in external signals too?

4. What's been the hardest part of getting the team to actually adopt these AI tools? Is it trust, data quality, workflow friction, something else?

## Account research and external context (15 min)

5. When an AE at ClickUp is preparing for a call or working a deal — what does their research workflow actually look like? Where do they go for information?

6. How much of that research is pulling from internal systems (Salesforce, Gong, email) vs. external sources (LinkedIn, company websites, news, G2)?

7. How long does that research typically take per account? Is there a difference between what top performers do vs. the average rep?

8. Where does the picture go stale? How quickly does the context an AE has about an account become outdated?

9. Have you tried to automate or streamline any of the external research piece? If so, what worked and what didn't?

10. Do your reps use any tools specifically for company/people intelligence today? (ZoomInfo, Apollo, Clay, LinkedIn Sales Nav, etc.) What's good and what's missing?

## Signals and timing (10 min)

11. How does your team decide *when* to reach out to an account? Is that driven by intent data, manual triggers, rep intuition, something else?

12. What external signals would be most valuable if you could get them reliably? (Hiring changes, funding, product launches, leadership moves, tech stack changes, etc.)

13. Is there a gap between the signals your team has access to and the signals that would actually change how they sell? What's missing?

## Building vs. buying (10 min)

14. You've built a lot of GTM infrastructure internally across your career. When it comes to external context and intelligence — is that something you'd want to build in-house or buy?

15. What would a product need to deliver for you to trust it over the internal tooling you could build? What's the bar?

16. If someone handed you a search engine that could answer "tell me everything relevant about this company and this person right now" with real-time web data — where would you plug that into your workflow first?

17. How do you think about the data freshness problem? Static databases (ZoomInfo-style) vs. real-time signals — does that distinction matter in practice for your team?

## AI and the future of GTM (5 min)

18. You're one of the people actually building AI into GTM right now. What's working better than expected? What's been disappointing?

19. Where do you think the biggest unlock is in the next 12 months for AI in GTM? Is it internal systems (better CRM automation, call analysis) or external context (better data about the world)?

20. If you could wave a magic wand and fix one thing about how your GTM team operates today, what would it be?

## Close (2 min)

- Is there anything I didn't ask about that you think I should be thinking about?
- Anyone else you'd recommend we talk to who's working on similar problems?
