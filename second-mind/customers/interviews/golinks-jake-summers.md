## Pain Points Identified

- **Salesforce admin** is the biggest daily pain — manually porting Gong notes into Salesforce after every call. Wants this automated.
- **Data freshness/accuracy** — ZoomInfo and Sales Nav data is often out of date, contacts no longer at companies, incorrect contact info.
- **No single tool does it all** — has to pull from ZoomInfo, Sales Nav, ChatGPT, web, and manually assemble into a spreadsheet.
- **Research for large accounts** still takes 1-2 hours — identifying right people, mapping org structure, understanding initiatives.
- **No standardized process** — each AE does research differently, nothing templated.

## Key Learnings

1. **AI has already solved 90% of the research pain for sophisticated AEs.** Jake went from reading 50-page 10Ks to 15-minute queries. The remaining pain is assembly/synthesis across tools, not research itself.
2. **Salesforce admin is a bigger pain than research** — 7 demos/day = 7 manual Salesforce updates. This is the "hair on fire" problem, not account research.
3. **Org chart research kicks in at ~200 employees.** Below that, there's usually one IT buyer and a CEO. Above that, multi-threading becomes necessary.

**Surprises:** The degree to which AI has already collapsed research time. "Days" → "15 minutes to 1-2 hours" is a massive shift that happened in the last ~2 years.
