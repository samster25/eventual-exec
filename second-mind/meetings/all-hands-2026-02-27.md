# All-Hands Meeting — February 27, 2026

**Memo by:** Sammy
**Source:** [[source-documents/memos/all-hands-memo-2026-02-27.md]]

## Summary

Sammy addressed the full team with an honest account of the company's journey and announced the Eventual direction.

### Key Messages
- **Acknowledged team morale challenges** from multiple direction changes. "If you're feeling tired, uncertain, or frustrated, that's not a failure of commitment."
- **Traced the learning arc:** Daft Cloud -> Hackathon -> Search Sprint -> Eventual. Four major learning cycles in ~8 weeks.
- **Announced Eventual direction:** Deep people + company search for GTM. "The conversations feel different. Not politely interested. Different in a way we haven't felt before."
- **Daft remains core OSS infrastructure.** Priority order: Eventual > Apple > ByteDance > Community.
- **Key message:** "The speed of our learning is our greatest asset."
- **Friday eng wrap-ups** to demo progress — product team shows what was built each week.

### What We Learned (Conviction)
- "Indexing but easier" is not a wedge.
- Generic data connectors aren't defensible.
- Deep, specific search for concrete workflows is where value is.
- External context is the greenfield — internal context will be solved by partners (Glean, MCP).
- Our skills compound: data infra, search, customer intuition all feed into Eventual.

### Structure Going Forward
- **Eventual team:** Sammy, Jay, Colin — building full-time.
- **Daft team:** Under Varun's leadership, supporting Eventual + Apple + ByteDance + community.
- **Financial position:** 3+ years of runway.
