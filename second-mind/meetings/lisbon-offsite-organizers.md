## Day 2 — Behind the Scenes

### The Journey (9:30-10:00am) — Sammy + Jay
- 30 minutes, not longer
- Focus on vision and direction, not specific goals or metrics
- Tone: honest about the journey (Daft Cloud -> Hackathon -> Search Sprint -> Eventual), hopeful about where we're headed
- This is not the Q2 planning session — keep it high-level

### Mirror Mirror (10:15-11:45am) — Varun
- Varun runs this his way — details are his to manage
- Needs ~1.5 hours total
- Materials: large Post-it sheets + markers

### Working Session Proposals (12:45-2:45pm) — All
- **First 30 min (12:45-1:15):** Sammy + Jay set context on Q2 outcomes. Paint the landscape — NeoLabs doing dedupe, partnership opportunities, where Daft can be useful. Frame the outcomes you want, not mandates.
- **Remaining 1.5 hours (1:15-2:45):** Review submitted proposals from the form. People who haven't submitted can pitch live (~5 min each). Group discusses, combines related topics (e.g., shuffles + checkpointing). Goal: finalize 4 tracks for Day 3.
- Key framing: this is bottoms-up planning. Leadership provides context and outcomes, team works backward from it.
- Expect to combine things — e.g., shuffles and checkpointing might be one session. 3-hour blocks are long enough for combined topics.

### Q2 Goal-Setting (3:00-5:30pm)
- Everyone together first. Sammy + Jay give ~30 min more context.
- Then full engineering team discusses priorities — including things that won't be working sessions (e.g., Daft UI).
- Sammy + Jay peel off around 3:45-4:00 to the partnerships/marketing side with Steffi.
- This is more free-flowing discussion and prioritization, not structured pitches.

### Dinner (6:30pm)
- Palacio Chiado — round table setup
- Varun runs the sharing activity before courses are served
- Wine + sharing → dinner flows naturally
- Group photo — remind people about business casual in the morning

### After Dinner — Finalize Day 3 Schedule
- Leadership (Sammy, Jay, Varun, Maggie) locks in Day 3
- Should be straightforward — only 4 sessions to schedule across 2 blocks
- Assign: session leaders, participants, locations (which Airbnb), and share with team that night or at 9am kickoff
- One track will be Eventual Entity Search (Sammy, Jay, Colin)
- Marketing/business track also separate from engineering
