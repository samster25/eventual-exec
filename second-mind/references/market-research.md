## GTM Technology Landscape

For full detail, see [Notion: GTM Technology Landscape](https://www.notion.so/314091bd8634806c955bc210f4fd71e2)

### Framework: The GTM Stack

| Stage | Function | Key Players |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| **Find** | Identify prospects and accounts | ZoomInfo, Apollo, Cognism, Lusha, Clay |
| **Attract** | Content, ads, brand awareness | HubSpot Marketing, Marketo, various |
| **Engage** | Outbound outreach, sequences | Outreach, SalesLoft, Apollo, 11x, Artisan |
| **Sell** | CRM, deal management, closing | Salesforce, HubSpot CRM, Gong, Clari |
| **Understand** | Analytics, intelligence, research | 6sense, Bombora, G2, Crunchbase |
| **Retain & Grow** | Customer success, expansion | Gainsight, ChurnZero, Vitally |
| **Connect** | Integration, orchestration, data | Clay, Workato, Zapier, Fivetran |

**Eventual's position:** We sit at the intersection of Find + Understand — providing deep intelligence that existing tools don't offer through agent-native delivery.
