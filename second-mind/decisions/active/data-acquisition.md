# $250K Data Acquisition Budget

**Status:** pending-approval
**Owner:** Sammy

## Proposed Budget Breakdown

| Category | Estimated Spend | What It Gets Us |
|----------|----------------|-----------------|
| People data (LinkedIn profiles, org charts) | $100-150K | Broader people coverage for ICP's target accounts. Enable "who should I talk to?" workflow. |
| Company data (firmographic, funding, technographic) | $50-75K | Fill gaps in Series C-Fortune 500 coverage. Expand beyond AI/ML beachhead. |
| Signal data (job postings, content, news) | $25-50K | Timing signals: who's hiring, publishing, expanding. Makes Eventual proactive, not reactive. |
| Scraping infrastructure + compute | $25K | Proprietary collection pipelines. Begin building the data moat. |

## Rationale
- Every week without coverage is a demo that misses.
- Data acquisition is the bottleneck to testing H2 (curated context vs. web search) and H3 (data should go deep on who our ICP sells to).
- Current dataset: ~1,000 companies, ~117K people profiles, concentrated in AI/ML.
- ICP's targets are Series C to Fortune 500 — we need coverage there to demo effectively.
- $250K is an investment in hypothesis velocity.
- Inclusive of 3+ years runway — does not materially change burn.

## Timeline
- Vendor outreach begins Week 1 (offsite).
- First data drops landing by Week 2-3.
- Coverage expansion based on H3 learnings in Weeks 4-6.
