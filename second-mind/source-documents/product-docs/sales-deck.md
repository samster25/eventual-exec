People/company deep search for AI-powered GTM.

## The Volume Play Stopped Working

Everyone has AI SDRs, Deep Research, automated sequences. Response rates have collapsed.

Great sales is still **great messaging + great timing**.

---

## The Cost

**$50K ACV** × missed timing = deals that went to a competitor who nailed the right moment.

---

## The current stack is too generic

ZoomInfo for firmographics, Clay for workflows, ChatGPT for research. None of them surface anything interesting enough to go the extra mile for your prospect.

> *"Hi Sarah, I noticed Acme Corp recently raised a Series B — congrats! As a VP of Sales at a fast-growing SaaS company, you're thinking about scaling your team..."*

Every inbox has 50 of these.

---

## What Has Changed

The best GTM Engineering teams (Ramp, Vercel) are buying access to as much rich context as possible to power their AI systems.

AI can now reason across messy data — not just structured databases:

- **External signal**: blogs, talks, filings, LinkedIn, GitHub
- **Internal signal**: your CRM, past emails, internal notes

Most powerful when paired.
