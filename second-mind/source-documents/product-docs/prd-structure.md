# Eventual PRD Structure

## 1. Executive Summary
What Eventual is, who it's for, why now

## 2. Problem Statement
- Agent Builders need customer context to improve their AI products
- Today's options: build it yourself (slow, painful) or go without (worse product)
- The B2B2B challenge: AB needs AC data, AC needs control, no one has time

## 3. Why Existing Solutions Fall Short
- RAG-as-a-service (Pinecone, Weaviate): single-tenant, no B2B2B model, no consent
- iPaaS (Zapier, Merge): CRUD-focused, not retrieval-focused, no agent-native design
- Raw MCP: no unified policies across sources, no virtual addressing, limited observability

## 4. Our Unique Insight
- Don't be a lossy bottleneck - be a navigation layer
- Virtual addressing: define resources upstream sources don't expose
- Vibes to deterministic: natural language policies become enforceable rules
- The unlock: more integrations + enterprise-grade policies = expanded TAM for Agent Builders
